Privacy Notice

When you submit a video, we collect your name/social handle (if provided), the show details you enter, and the video file itself. This information is used solely to credit your submission and curate the fan gallery.

We do not sell your data. Your name and handle are displayed only if your clip is approved and you have provided them. You may request removal of your submission at any time by contacting us.

Video files are stored securely on third-party cloud storage (e.g., Cloudinary or AWS S3). We retain footage for as long as the fan archive is active.
